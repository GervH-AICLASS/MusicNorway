Popular Media Inspired by Norwegian Culture, Norse Mythology, and Folklore

Norway's rich cultural heritage, including its Norse mythology and folklore, has inspired various forms of media worldwide. From TV shows and movies to books and video games, these stories continue to captivate audiences with their unique blend of ancient legends and modern storytelling.

TV Series: Vikings (2013-2020)

"Vikings" is a historical drama series that draws heavily from Norse mythology and the Viking Age. While it's not strictly a Norwegian production, it significantly features Norwegian history and culture. The show follows the legendary Viking Ragnar Lothbrok and his family as they navigate the complexities of Norse society and venture into new lands.

  • Medium: TV Series
  • Genres: Historical Drama, Action, Adventure
  • Seasons: 6
  • Created by: Michael Hirst
  • Key themes: Norse mythology, Viking culture, exploration
  • Notable characters: Ragnar Lothbrok, Lagertha, Bjorn Ironside, Ivar the Boneless

The series is known for its blend of historical events and mythological elements, offering viewers a glimpse into the beliefs, customs, and daily life of the Viking Age. While it takes some creative liberties, "Vikings" has been praised for its production values, performances, and its role in sparking renewed interest in Norse history and culture.

TV Series: Ragnarok (2020-2023)

This Netflix original series is a modern reimagining of Norse mythology set in the fictional Norwegian town of Edda. The show follows a teenager who discovers he's the reincarnation of Thor, the Norse god of thunder, and must fight against ancient giants disguised as wealthy industrialists.

  • Medium: TV Series
  • Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Thriller
  • Seasons: 3
  • Created by: Adam Price

Video Game: God of War (2018)

While not created in Norway, this popular video game heavily features Norse mythology. The story follows Kratos, a Greek demigod, as he ventures into the Norse realms with his son Atreus. The game incorporates many elements of Norse mythology, including gods, creatures, and locations.

  • Medium: Video Game
  • Genre: Action-adventure
  • Developer: Santa Monica Studio
  • Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment

Book: Norse Mythology by Neil Gaiman (2017)

This bestselling book retells classic Norse myths in a novelistic style. While not Norwegian, Gaiman's work has helped popularize these stories for a modern audience and has been widely read in Norway and beyond.

  • Medium: Book
  • Genre: Mythology, Fiction
  • Author: Neil Gaiman

Movie: Troll (2022)

This Norwegian film blends folklore with modern action. When an ancient troll is awakened in a Norwegian mountain, a group of heroes must come together to try and stop it from wreaking deadly havoc.

  • Medium: Movie
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy
  • Director: Roar Uthaug
  • Available on: Netflix

Impact of Norwegian Culture on Global Media

These examples demonstrate the enduring influence of Norwegian culture, Norse mythology, and folklore on contemporary global media. By reimagining ancient stories in modern settings or retelling them for new audiences, these works keep Norwegian traditions alive and introduce them to people worldwide. The success of these various media forms has also helped showcase Norway's rich storytelling heritage and its impact on global popular culture.
